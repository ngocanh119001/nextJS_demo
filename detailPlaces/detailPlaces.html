Ba Na Hills in Da Nang
Hoa Vang, Da Nang, Viet Nam Open | Wed, 08:00-22:00

What You'll Experience

Explore Ba Na Hills, one of the most popular attractions in Da Nang
Hitch a ride on Ba Na Hills' cable car network ranked by CNN in 2017 as one of the world's 10 most impressive cable car infrastructure systems Have the time of your life at Fantasy Park located at an elevation of 1,489 meters above sea level Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of Ba Na Hills from the Golden Bridge which was voted by TIME magazine in 2018 as one of the world's 100 greatest places.
Whether it's your first time in Da Nang or you've been here many times before, Ba Na Hills should always be on your itinerary. To get to Ba Na Hills, you will hitch a ride on the park's cable car network and glide over the stunning mountains and forests. Once you get to the park, the fun begins. There's Fantasy Park, an entertainment hub with more than 105 arcade machines and attractions. There's French Village, a village built with distinct French Medieval architectural style, which provides the perfect background for any photoshoot. And then there's the park's crème de la crème — the Golden Bridge. Voted by TIME magazine in 2018 as one of the world's 100 greatest places, the bridge is where you play the audience to breathtaking panoramic views of Ba Na Hills. So what are you waiting for?
