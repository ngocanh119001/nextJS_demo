Ba Na Hills
Ba Na Hills is renowned for its stunning natural landscapes, entertainment areas such as Sun World Ba Na Hills, and the Golden Bridge - a prominent golden bridge in the sky.
Marble Mountains (Ngũ Hành Sơn)
The Marble Mountains are a famous group of limestone mountains, featuring caves, marble caves, and spiritual sites such as temples and shrines.
My Khe Beach
My Khe Beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Da Nang, with fine white sand, clear blue water, and vibrant entertainment services.
Dragon Bridge (Cau Rong)
The Dragon Bridge is an iconic symbol of Da Nang, with unique architecture and a nightly fireworks show.
Linh Ung Pagoda
Linh Ung Pagoda is famous for its largest bronze Buddha statue in Vietnam and the breathtaking natural landscape from above.
